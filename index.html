About Me

I am originally from Seattle, Washington and my goal is to become a software programmer. I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in psychology and a Master of Business Administation from the University of Florida.

I have worked the past three years at Otis Elevator Company as an Account Manager, overseeing over 300 accounts and consistently growing my territory.

I decided to switch to programming due to three reasons:

  1. I did not enjoy my work, nor did I have a sense of accomplishment or fulfillment when I did well
  2. The job market for my position is few and far between, while programmers are always being sought after
  3. My friend also did not study computer science in college, but he taught himself and was able to become a programmer and it has inspired me to do the same
